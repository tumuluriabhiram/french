🗣️ Practice Sentences (Clique pour écouter)

Lundi à 8h, j’ai les maths.

(On Monday at 8 o’clock, I have maths.)

Mardi à 10h30, j’ai l’anglais.

(On Tuesday at 10:30, I have English.)

Mercredi à midi, j’ai la science.

(On Wednesday at noon, I have science.)

Jeudi à 14h, j’ai l’histoire.

(On Thursday at 2 PM, I have history.)

Vendredi à 9h15, j’ai le français.

(On Friday at 9:15, I have French.)

Lundi à 13h, j’ai l’EPS.

(On Monday at 1 PM, I have PE.)

❓ Quand as-tu les maths ?

👉 J’ai les maths lundi à 8h.

📖 Useful Structures:

🔹 À + time = at (e.g., À 8h)

🔹 Lundi / Mardi... = Days (e.g., Mardi à 10h)

🔹 J’ai + subject = I have (e.g., J’ai les maths)

🗣️ Talking about Classes:

There are two ways to say "I have...":

1️⃣ J’ai + (le/la/les) + subject

Ex: J’ai les maths. / J'ai le français.

2️⃣ J’ai cours de + subject

Ex: J’ai cours de maths. / J'ai cours de français.

💡 Grammar Tip: Le Lundi vs Lundi

🔹 Lundi = Monday (this specific Monday)

Ex: Lundi, j'ai l'anglais.

🔹 Le lundi = On Mondays (every Monday / habit)

Ex: Le lundi, j'ai la natation.

🗼 Culture : Les Monuments de Paris

🗼

La Tour Eiffel

The most iconic symbol of France. It was built by Gustave Eiffel for the 1889 World's Fair.

🖼️

Le Musée du Louvre

The world's largest art museum and a historic monument in Paris, home to the Mona Lisa.

⛪

Notre-Dame de Paris

A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture located on the Île de la Cité.

⚔️

Les Invalides

A historic complex of buildings related to France's military history, containing the tomb of Napoleon Bonaparte.

🏛️

L'Arc de Triomphe

One of the most famous monuments in Paris, standing at the western end of the Champs-Élysées.

⛪

Le Sacré-Cœur

A white Roman Catholic church dedicated to the Sacred Heart of Jesus, located at the highest point in the city (Montmartre).
