⏰ Time Expressions with Days

Talking About Your Schedule

Le lundi, j'ai maths.

On Mondays, I have math.

Use "le" + day for "every" that day

Lundi, j'ai maths.

On Monday (this Monday), I have math.

No "le" for a specific day

J'ai cours de français.

I have French class.

"Cours de" = class of/in
