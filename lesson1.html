👥 Tu vs Vous

👫 TU (Informal)

👫
🐕

Use for friends, family, and pets!

Example: "Salut! Comment vas-tu?"

👨‍🏫 VOUS (Formal)

👨‍🏫
🧑‍💼

Use for teachers, adults, and strangers.

Example: "Bonjour! Comment allez-vous?"

😊 Comment ça va? (How are you?)

Comment vas-tu? INFORMAL
How are you?
Tu vas bien? INFORMAL
Are you doing well?
Comment ça va? / Ça va? How's it going? (Very common)
Comment allez-vous? FORMAL
How are you?
Vous allez bien? FORMAL
Are you doing well?

💬 La Réponse (The Response)

Typical ways to answer "Comment ça va?":

Je vais bien. 😊
I am doing well.
Ça va bien. ✨
Things are going well.
Ça va. 👍
I'm fine / It's going okay.
Comme ci, comme ça. ↔️
So-so / Not too bad.
Ça va mal. 😔
Things are going badly.

Comment ça s’épelle? How do you spell it?

A polite exchange between Paul and Madame Sophie.

Paul

(Good morning Madam. How are you?)

(Good morning Sir. I am doing well, thank you. And you?)

  • 🌍 North America (Canada)
