Ils/Elles ont (They have)

Être (To be)

Je suis (I am)
Tu es (You are)
Il/Elle/On est (He/She/One is)
Nous sommes (We are)
Vous êtes (You are)
Ils/Elles sont (They are)

💡 Focus on "On"

What it means:

  • On = Nous (We)
  • On = we / one / people

How to conjugate:

Even though it often means "we", we conjugate it exactly like il or elle!

  • Il a = he has
  • Elle a = she has
  • On a = we have / one has
