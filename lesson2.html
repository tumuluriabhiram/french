🔢 Les Nombres de 61 à 100

💡 The French Counting System

French numbers use math after 60! Pay attention to the patterns:
• 70-79: Continue counting from 60 + 10. (e.g., 70 = soixante-dix, 71 = soixante et onze)
• 80-89: Count as 4 x 20. (e.g., 80 = quatre-vingts, 81 = quatre-vingt-un)
• 90-99: Count as 80 + 10 to 19. (e.g., 90 = quatre-vingt-dix, 91 = quatre-vingt-onze)
