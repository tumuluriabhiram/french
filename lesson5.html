🇫🇷 Culture & Civilisation : Les Fêtes et les Jours Fériés en France

Learn about important holidays and celebrations in France!

🎆

Le Jour de l'An

Le 1er Janvier

New Year's Day. French people celebrate with a "Réveillon" (big dinner) and wish each other "Bonne Année!"

🌸

La Fête du Travail

Le 1er Mai

Labor Day. It is a tradition to offer "du muguet" (lily of the valley) to friends and family for good luck.

🥚🐰

Pâques

Mars ou Avril

Easter. Pâques celebrates the resurrection of Jesus. Children hunt for chocolate eggs and bells. Families often eat a traditional meal of lamb (gigot d'agneau).

🇫🇷

La Fête Nationale

Le 14 Juillet

Bastille Day. France's National Day celebrated with military parades on the Champs-Élysées and fireworks all over the country.

🎄

Noël

Le 25 Décembre

Christmas Day. A time for family gatherings, big meals (like "La Bûche de Noël"), and exchanging gifts.

🕊️

La Toussaint

Le 1er Novembre

All Saints' Day. A day to honor all saints and remember loved ones who have passed away.
